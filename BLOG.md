# Automate Aptos Blockchain Operations with Our New n8n Community Node

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-aptos, a powerful community node that brings Aptos blockchain capabilities directly into your n8n workflows!

## The Challenge

As blockchain technology continues to evolve, developers and businesses working with Aptos face a common challenge: integrating blockchain operations into their existing automation workflows. Whether you're managing multiple accounts, processing transactions, or monitoring blockchain activity, manual processes are time-consuming and error-prone.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-aptos

Velocity BPA has developed a comprehensive solution that bridges the gap between Aptos blockchain and n8n's powerful automation platform. Our new community node enables you to seamlessly integrate Aptos blockchain operations into your workflows without writing complex code.

## Key Features

The n8n-nodes-aptos node provides four essential operations:

**Account Management**: Create and manage Aptos accounts directly within your workflows. Perfect for onboarding processes or multi-account management scenarios.

**Transaction Execution**: Submit and execute transactions on the Aptos blockchain with ease. Automate complex transaction workflows that previously required manual intervention.

**Coin Transfers**: Facilitate APT coin transfers between accounts seamlessly. Ideal for payment processing, distribution workflows, or treasury management.

**Block Queries**: Retrieve and monitor blockchain data by querying specific blocks. Essential for analytics, monitoring, and audit trail purposes.

## Getting Started

Installing the node is straightforward. Simply run:


npm install n8n-nodes-aptos


Once installed, restart your n8n instance, and you'll find the Aptos node available in your node palette, ready to integrate into your workflows.
